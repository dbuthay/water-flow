   - Click "Add Resource"
   - URL: `/local/irrigation-monitor-card.js`
   - Type: `JavaScript Module`
   - Click "Create"

2. **Adding the card to a dashboard:**
   - Edit a dashboard, click "Add Card"
   - Search for "Irrigation Monitor" in the card picker
   - Or use manual YAML configuration:
     ```yaml
     type: custom:irrigation-monitor-card
     title: "My Irrigation"  # optional, defaults to "Irrigation Monitor"
     ```

3. **What the card shows:**
   - All monitored zones auto-discovered (no entity configuration needed)
   - Zone tiles with state indicator (idle=grey, running=blue, leak=red)
   - Current flow rate for active zones
   - Today's water usage per zone
